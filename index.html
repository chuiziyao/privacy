HammerPhotoOrganizer Privacy Policy | 隐私政策

1. Information We Collect

HammerPhotoOrganizer is a local photo management tool that processes data only on your device. We do not actively collect, upload, or share your personal information to any third-party servers.

  • Locally Accessed Information: The app accesses photos, image files, and their metadata (e.g., capture time, device model) on your device solely for displaying, categorizing, and managing your photos within the app.
  • Device Information: The app may read necessary device information (e.g., OS version, screen resolution) only to ensure proper functionality and compatibility, and will not use it to identify your personal identity.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • All photos and related data are stored only on your local device or the local storage path you specify. No data is uploaded to the cloud or third-party servers.
  • We will never use your personal information for advertising, analytics, or other non-essential purposes.

3. Information Sharing & Disclosure

We do not share, sell, or rent your personal information to any third parties, except in the following cases:

  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose necessary information in compliance with laws, legal processes, or government requests.
  • Safety & Protection: We may disclose necessary information to protect our or your legitimate rights, interests, and safety.

4. Your Rights

  • You may view, edit, or delete your photos and related data at any time via your system file manager or in-app features.
  • You may uninstall the app at any time, and all locally stored data will be removed along with the app.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical measures to protect your local data. However, since data is stored on your device, you are responsible for securing your device (e.g., setting a lock screen password, backing up data regularly).

6. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will be posted on this page. For material changes, we will notify you via in-app notifications or other reasonable means.
