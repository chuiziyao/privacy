Privacy Policy for HammerPhotoOrganizer

Last updated: March 21, 2026

1. Information Collection

HammerPhotoOrganizer is a local desktop application. We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information, photos, videos, or user data to external servers. All file operations (sorting, backup, deduplication, EXIF editing) are performed entirely on your local device.

2. Data Usage

All photo/video files are processed locally only for the functions you initiate (e.g., organizing by date, removing duplicates). No data is uploaded to cloud services, shared with third parties, or used for analytics.

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We will never share, sell, rent, or disclose any of your data (including local files) to any third party under any circumstances.

4. Privacy & Security

Since no data leaves your device, your privacy and data security are fully protected. You retain full control over all your photos and videos at all times.
